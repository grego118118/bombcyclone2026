Home Maintenance & Defense

Proactive steps to protect your property from record snowfall and sub-zero temperatures.

Preventing Frozen Pipes

When temperatures drop below 20Â°F, pipes are at high risk of bursting.

  • Drip the Faucets: Even a tiny trickle of water helps prevent pipes from freezing.
  • Open Cabinet Doors: Allow heat to reach pipes under sinks in kitchens and bathrooms.
  • Insulate Exposed Pipes: Use foam sleeves on pipes in attics or crawlspaces.

Roof & Gutter Safety

Heavy snow accumulation can put immense stress on your home's structure.

  • Clear Gutter Downspouts: Prevents ice dams from forming and forcing water into your walls.
  • Use a Roof Rake: Safely remove snow from the edge of your roof while standing on the ground.
  • Watch for Leaks: Check ceilings for damp spots daily during the thaw.
