Vehicle Safety & Emergency Prep

If you must travel during Winter Storm Gianna, ensure your vehicle is an asset, not a liability.

Essential Vehicle Kit

Pack these items immediately. They can be life-savers if you become stranded.

  • Shovel & Traction: A small snow shovel and a bag of sand or kitty litter.
  • Jump Starter: Cold temperatures drain batteries rapidly.
  • Warmth: Multi-purpose wool blankets and hand warmers.

Driving Protocol

Safety rules for navigating blizzard or icy conditions.

  • Gas Level: Keep your tank at least half full to prevent fuel line freeze-up.
  • Exhaust Pipe: If stranded, ensure the exhaust pipe is clear of snow before running the engine for heat.
  • Stay with Vehicle: If stuck, do not walk away in a blizzard. Your car is your primary shelter.

Winter Tire Efficiency

Ensure your tires have adequate tread. If traveling into the 12"+ snow zones of SC or Boston, consider chains or snow-rated tires.
